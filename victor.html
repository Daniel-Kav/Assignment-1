Lobster Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 lobsters (1 1/2 to 2 pounds each)
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Split the lobsters in half lengthwise using a sharp knife, and remove the green tomalley and roe if desired.
  4. Brush the lobster flesh with the butter mixture.
  5. Place the lobsters flesh side down on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes until the flesh is opaque and lightly charred.
  6. Flip the lobsters shell side down and grill for an additional 4-5 minutes until the shells are bright red and the meat is fully cooked.
  7. Remove from the grill and serve immediately with additional melted butter and lemon wedges if desired.

Enjoy your delicious grilled lobster!
